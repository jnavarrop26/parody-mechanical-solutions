## About Us

Parody Mechanical Solutions LLC is a contractor company specialized in providing comprehensive technical support for commercial fleets, heavy equipment, and industrial transportation. We focus on preventive maintenance, rapid troubleshooting and solutions (troubleshooting), and on-site assistance for critical mechanical failures directly in the field, infrastructure projects, or roadside emergency service.

**Maintenance badges:** Preventive · Corrective · Predictive

## Core Competencies

1. **System Diagnostics & Scanning** — Real-time monitoring of data lines and flows, active fault code detection, and restoration of vehicle communication networks.
2. **Electrical Problem Solving** — Tracing and repair of short circuits, advanced alternator, connector, sensor, and automotive & industrial relay diagnostics.
3. **High-Pressure Hydraulic Systems** — Diagnostics and repair of leaks in lines, hoses, pressure pumps, hydraulic cylinders, valves, and heavy equipment control systems.
4. **Fleet General Mechanics** — Corrective and preventive mechanical services for heavy-duty diesel trucks, gasoline and utility vehicles.
5. **On-Site Mobile Support** — Deployment of fully equipped mobile units with advanced tools to solve technical issues in hard-to-access work areas or remote projects.
6. **Roadside Mechanical Assistance (Roadside Support)** — Rapid response on highways and transport routes to minimize commercial vehicle downtime and heavy equipment.
7. **Salvage & Component Recovery** — Recovery, disassembly and evaluation of parts and reusable components to reduce costs and extend the useful life of equipment.

## Maintenance Services

### Preventive Maintenance
- Scheduled inspections
- Fluid and systems analysis
- Lubrication and adjustments
- Replacement of critical components
- Reports and recommendations

### Corrective Maintenance
- Diagnostics and failure resolution
- On-site or in-shop repairs
- Replacement of defective parts
- System restoration
- Functional testing and verification

### Predictive Maintenance
- Real-time parameter monitoring
- Data and trend analysis
- Early fault detection
- Reduction of equipment downtime
- Extended equipment life
